How to Bake Chocolate Chip Cookies

Baking cookies is a fun and simple task. Follow these steps to make warm, delicious chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth
  3. Beat eggs in, one at a time, then stir in vanilla extract.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  5. Stir in flour and then fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto an ungreased baking sheet.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until the edges are just starting to brown, about 10 minutes.

Let cookies cool on a wire rack, then enjoy!
