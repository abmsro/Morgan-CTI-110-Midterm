How to Bake Chocolate Chip Cookies

Baking cookies is a fun and simple task. Follow these steps to make warm, delicious chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Mix flour and baking soda in a small bowl.
  3. Cream butter and sugars together in a large bowl.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix well.
  5. Slowly add flour mixture to wet ingredients.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 9–11 minutes until golden brown.

Let cookies cool, then enjoy!
